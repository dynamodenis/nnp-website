OUR CONSULTANTS

Grace Migwi

Grace Migwi is a self-motivated professional, with over 15 year’s expertise in Agricultural education, Research, Innovation and technology transfer. She holds a Master’s degree in Agricultural Education from Egerton University.

Areas of Expertise:

  • Consultancy in research and training.
  • Agricultural innovation generation and technology transfer.
  • Agricultural education curriculum development and preparation of training modules.
  • Capacity building for farmers and associations.

She is a trainer in Agro-processing in Nyeri National Polytechnic and has coordinated various local and international projects such as:-

  • Young Africa Works.
  • Supporting Innovation in Vocational Education Sector (SITVES).
  • Kenya Education For Employment Programme.
  • Lorna Young Foundation.
